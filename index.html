Welcome to Bites & Delights

We are a small café and bakeshop dedicated to serving the Dulag community—especially students and local workers. Whether you are looking for a quick grab-and-go snack or a cozy space to study and hang out with friends, we have a seat saved just for you.


Enjoy modern convenience! We offer Cashless Payments (GCash/Maya) and a QR Code Menu for a fast, hassle-free experience.
